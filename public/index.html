Project Bluefish

Project Bluefish brings an immersive BLS experience from your browser. Explore BLS as you would in real life.

Made from scratch

Project Bluefish was made with Blender, an open source 3D modeling software. This model was built from the ground up through reference photos, maps, and floorplans. With computer-aided design, we were able to recreate some of the most unique aspects of BLS.
